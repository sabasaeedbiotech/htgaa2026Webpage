## Your HTGAA site

This is the top level of your HTGAA site - this diretory contains your homepage and directories inside it contain all of the other pages on your site.\
(We recommend you bookmark this page so you can come directly here to edit.)

Click [_index.md](_index.md) here (or above) to edit the homepage of your
HTGAA site, or click [homework](homework), [labs](labs), or [projects](projects) to work on those.\

and refer to the [HTGAA Markdown Editing Guide](https://pages.htgaa.org/2026a/course-pages/editing-guide/) for tips
on markdown, images, tools, and more.
